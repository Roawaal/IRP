Brexit officially took place at 23:00 GMT on 31 January 2020 (00:00 1 February 2020 CET). After 4 years, British people are coming to realize the effects of Brexit on their own lives and livelihoods.

According to the survey, some 70% of the farmers who grow cereals said Brexit had been negative for their businesses, while 76% of oilseed rape growers felt that way. Similarly, 68% of farmers with beef cattle, dairy cows or sheep had a negative perception. FarmersWeekly 23 Jun 2023

This project aims to explore Brexit's wide-ranging effects on agricultral using interactive spatial data analysis technologies to sense and predict the trends, analyze the dynamic changes in social media topics and sentiments, understanding public opinion trends.
